Asmbly Labs is infrastructure for distributed manufacturing, focused on 3D printing. We connect sellers, makers, and designers to enable local fulfillment of physical products. When a customer places an order, we route it to a nearby maker who 3D prints and ships the item locally.

Sellers who want to offer physical products without inventory, makers with 3D printers looking to earn income, and designers who want to license their STL files for royalties. The platform works with Shopify, TikTok Shop, WooCommerce, and other e-commerce platforms.

No. Sellers can offer 3D printed products without owning equipment or holding inventory. Our network of makers handles all production and shipping. If you're a maker, you do need reliable 3D printing equipment to fulfill orders.

Our system automatically routes orders to qualified makers based on location, material capabilities, quality standards, and capacity. This ensures customers receive their orders from the closest available maker who can meet the requirements.

If a maker encounters issues with production, the order is automatically rerouted to another qualified maker in the network. We maintain quality standards and backup capacity to ensure orders are completed reliably.

Fulfillment speed depends on print complexity and local maker capacity. Simple items can often be completed within 1-3 days, while complex or large items may take longer. Local production means shorter shipping distances and faster delivery to customers.

Sellers receive their profit margin after production and platform fees. Makers earn for each completed order based on material, complexity, and time. Designers receive royalties when their licensed STL files are used. All payouts are processed regularly and transparently.
