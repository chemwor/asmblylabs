Privacy Policy

Asmbly Labs is a distributed manufacturing platform operated by Asmbly Labs, Inc. We connect sellers, makers, and designers in a secure 3D printing network. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information with the highest standards of security and transparency.

Asmbly Labs Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you participate in our distributed manufacturing platform, including when you visit our website, register as a user, or engage in manufacturing, selling, or design activities through Asmbly Labs.

Effective Date: December 14, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to facilitate distributed manufacturing operations, ensure quality standards, process payments, and maintain platform security. The information we collect varies based on your role as a Seller, Maker, or Designer.

Account and Identity Information
  • Personal Details – Name, email address, phone number, and location information for order routing and communication
  • Payment Information – Bank account details, tax identification numbers, and payout preferences for revenue distribution
  • Verification Data – Identity verification documents and business registration information as required for platform participation
Manufacturing and Equipment Information
  • Equipment Specifications – 3D printer models, material capabilities, print volume specifications, and quality certifications
  • Production Capacity – Availability schedules, production timelines, and fulfillment capabilities
  • Quality Metrics – Order completion rates, quality scores, and customer feedback ratings
Design and Intellectual Property Information
  • STL Files – Design files uploaded by designers, including file metadata and technical specifications
  • Licensing Terms – Usage rights, royalty rates, and distribution permissions for design files
  • Ownership Documentation – Proof of design ownership and intellectual property rights verification

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to operate our distributed manufacturing platform, facilitate order routing, ensure quality standards, process payments, and maintain platform security.

Order Processing and Fulfillment
  • Route orders to appropriate makers based on location, equipment capabilities, and material requirements
  • Coordinate production schedules and delivery timelines between sellers and makers
  • Track order progress and provide status updates to all parties
  • Facilitate communication between sellers, makers, and designers regarding order specifications
Payment and Revenue Distribution
  • Process payments from customers and distribute revenue to sellers, makers, and designers
  • Calculate royalty payments for STL file usage and licensing fees
  • Generate tax documentation and transaction records as required
  • Manage dispute resolution and payment holds when necessary
Quality Assurance and Platform Security
  • Monitor production quality and maintain maker certification standards
  • Verify user identities and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Protect STL files and intellectual property through secure distribution systems
  • Analyze platform performance and optimize order routing algorithms

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share information only as necessary to facilitate distributed manufacturing operations and comply with legal requirements.

Within Our Platform Network
  • • Order details shared between sellers and assigned makers for fulfillment
  • • Designer information shared with makers when STL files are licensed for production
  • • Quality ratings and feedback shared to maintain platform standards
  • • Geographic and capability information used for order routing optimization
Third-Party Service Providers
  • • Payment processors for secure financial transactions
  • • Cloud storage providers for secure STL file hosting
  • • Identity verification services for user authentication
  • • Communication platforms for order coordination

Data Security and Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information and maintain the integrity of our distributed manufacturing network.

STL File Security
  • • End-to-end encryption for file transfers
  • • Access-controlled distribution to authorized makers only
  • • Automatic file deletion after order completion
  • • Digital rights management and usage tracking
Payment and Financial Data
  • • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • • Encrypted storage of banking information
  • • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • • Regular security audits and monitoring
Manufacturing Data
  • • Secure transmission of order specifications
  • • Protected storage of quality and performance metrics
  • • Controlled access to production schedules and capabilities
  • • Privacy-preserving analytics for platform optimization

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information and can manage your privacy preferences through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

Access and Updates
  • • View and update your profile information
  • • Modify equipment specifications and capabilities
  • • Update payment and payout preferences
  • • Review order history and platform activity
Data Portability and Deletion
  • • Request export of your platform data
  • • Delete STL files and design portfolios
  • • Close account and remove personal information
  • • Retain transaction records as legally required

International Data Transfers

Our distributed manufacturing network operates globally, which may require transferring your information across international borders to facilitate order fulfillment and platform operations.

  • Cross-Border Manufacturing – Order routing may involve makers in different countries based on proximity, capabilities, and delivery requirements
  • Data Protection Standards – We implement appropriate safeguards and comply with applicable data protection regulations in all operating regions
  • Regional Compliance – Platform operations adhere to GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations based on user location

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our platform operations, legal requirements, or privacy practices. We will notify users of significant changes through platform notifications and email communications.

  • Notification Process – Users will receive advance notice of material policy changes affecting their rights or data usage
  • Continued Participation – Continued use of the platform after policy updates indicates acceptance of revised terms
  • Opt-Out Options – Users who disagree with policy changes may close their accounts before the effective date
