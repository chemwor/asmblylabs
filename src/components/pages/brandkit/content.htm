Asmbly Labs

Traditional e-commerce limits sellers to existing inventory and expensive upfront manufacturing costs. Asmbly Labs changes this with a 3D printing marketplace that connects sellers to an endless catalog of print-on-demand products. Designers upload STL files, 3D printer makers fulfill orders, and sellers expand their product offerings without inventory risk—all working as one collaborative system.

For sellers, this means instant access to thousands of unique, customizable products that can be printed and shipped directly to customers. No warehousing, no minimum orders, no upfront costs—just pure profit potential. For designers, it's a platform to monetize their creativity with fair revenue sharing. For makers with 3D printers, it's steady income from their equipment and skills.

The platform seamlessly integrates with existing e-commerce stores, automatically syncing product catalogs, managing orders, and coordinating fulfillment through our network of verified 3D printing makers. When a customer places an order, the nearest qualified maker prints and ships the product, while revenue is fairly distributed across all contributors.

Built on shared ownership and transparent systems, Asmbly Labs ensures everyone benefits: sellers gain competitive advantages with unique product offerings, designers earn from every print of their STL files, and makers build sustainable businesses around their 3D printing capabilities. It's not just a marketplace—it's a cooperative ecosystem that scales together.

Whether you're an e-commerce seller looking to expand your catalog, a designer wanting to monetize your STL files, or a maker ready to turn your 3D printer into a profitable business, Asmbly Labs provides the tools and network to succeed in the growing print-on-demand economy.
